Co-Housing

A More Affordable Way To Live In Prague:

  • Shared House -- Similar systems work in several countries. Mietshäusersyndikat (Apartment House Syndicate) in neighbouring Germany is by far the most advanced. It is an alliance of more than 120 apartment houses. A relatively recent network established in Austria, Habitat, with two housing projects in nearby Linz and in Salzburg, is also our great inspiration.
  • What is the Shared Houses network? Basically, it is a network of houses providing its residents affordable and community‑based accommodation in collective ownership.
